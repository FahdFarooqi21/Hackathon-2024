About Us

We are a group of students dedicated to making a positive impact by addressing food waste and helping fellow students. EcoEats was created with a vision to support students facing financial challenges while promoting sustainable food habits.

Our Roadmap for the Future

As EcoEats grows, we aim to establish partnerships with GS1 to access their barcode-scanning API, which uses Global Trade Item Numbers for accurate product data. This will complement Open Food Facts, which may lack data for certain items.

We also plan to collaborate with popular supermarkets like Morrisons and Aldi, where many students do their grocery shopping. This partnership would make it easier for students to access affordable and sustainable food options.

To ensure safety, our initial launch will not include direct staff support. However, we envision adding a separate section for university staff, from lecturers to janitors, in the future to promote community-wide sustainability.

To enhance security, we plan to implement VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing) alongside student ID verification.
